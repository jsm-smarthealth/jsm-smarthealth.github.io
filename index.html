SERVICES

DATA

We provide intuitive user interfaces and API integration possibilities that collect data from existing HIS, EMRs, Diagnostic Machines, IoT-based community monitoring devices, personal health monitoring devices, mobile Apps, health record vaults, etc., and feeds it into the data engine.

KNOWLEDGE

We process the available data – convert the unstructured text into structured data, add a layer of modelling and predictive analytics to it, and set the foundation for creating customised solutions with specific benefits for multiple stakeholders across the healthcare spectrum.

ACTION

This stage involves suggesting data-driven relevant actions to multiple stakeholders of the platform – management, care-providers, and more. The insights drawn from the data enhance revenue sources, exploration and research, clinical decisions, and other key factors.

One patient. One health record.

A platform that delivers clinical and business benefits from patient records, across settings, across time.

At SmartHealth, we believe that the company culture is, not at all, a mechanical engineering process. It is much more akin to gardening because a company is a living organism and not a machine. In other words, if we get the conditions and the environment right at SmartHealth then all of us – employees, management and shareholders alike – can flourish and bloom.

We passionately believe that the intelligence of the collective is much more than the intelligence of the management team.
